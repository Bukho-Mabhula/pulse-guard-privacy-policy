# pulse-guard-privacy-policy

**Effective date:** 01/04/2026 
**Last updated:** 06/07/2026

Pulse Guard respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Pulse Guard mobile application, website, and related services.

By using Pulse Guard, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. About Pulse Guard

Pulse Guard is a safety reporting app that allows users to record and submit video reports, share incident location information, receive safety notifications, view heatmap information, and track the status of submitted reports.

Pulse Guard may process sensitive information, including video, audio, location data, and incident-related details.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Account Information

When you register or use the app, we may collect information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Login or authentication details
- User profile information

### Incident Report Information

When you submit a report, we may collect:

- Video recorded through the app
- Audio captured with the video
- GPS location attached to the report
- Date and time of the report
- Incident status information
- Report acknowledgement or resolution updates
- Any other information submitted with the report

### Device and Technical Information

We may collect technical information such as:

- Device type
- Operating system
- App version
- Network information
- Push notification token
- Diagnostic logs
- Error reports
- Usage and performance data

### Location Information

Pulse Guard may collect your location when needed for app features, including incident reporting, map display, heatmap functionality, and safety alerts.

## 3. Camera, Microphone, Location, and Notification Permissions

Pulse Guard may request access to:

- Camera, to record incident videos
- Microphone, to include audio with video reports
- Location, to attach location to reports and support map-based features
- Notifications, to send safety alerts and report status updates

You can manage these permissions through your device settings. If you disable permissions, some app features may not work correctly.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Operate and improve Pulse Guard
- Submit and process incident reports
- Attach location information to reports
- Display safety maps and heatmaps
- Show report progress, acknowledgement, or resolution status
- Send safety notifications and alerts
- Support authorised safety or response personnel
- Prevent misuse, fraud, abuse, or unlawful activity
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Maintain app security and reliability
- Comply with legal obligations

## 5. Report Status and Acknowledgement

Pulse Guard may show whether a report has been sent, received, acknowledged, resolved, or otherwise updated.

These status updates are based on information available through Pulse Guard systems or connected response workflows. A status update does not guarantee emergency response, police response, medical response, or any specific outcome.

## 6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

- Authorised safety personnel or administrators
- Relevant response teams or authorities
- Cloud hosting and storage providers
- Map, notification, analytics, and infrastructure providers
- Service providers who help operate Pulse Guard
- Legal or regulatory authorities where required by law
- Parties involved in investigating misuse, abuse, or security incidents

Information shared may include video, audio, location data, incident details, report status, account information, and technical information where necessary.

## 7. Third-Party Services

Pulse Guard may use third-party services for hosting, storage, maps, notifications, analytics, infrastructure, and security.

These third-party services may process information only as needed to provide services to Pulse Guard. Their use of information may also be governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide Pulse Guard services
- Maintain incident records
- Support safety response workflows
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Prevent misuse or fraud
- Improve app reliability and security

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the purpose for which it is used.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information submitted through Pulse Guard.

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee that information will always be protected from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, disclosure, or alteration.

## 10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

- Access personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of personal information
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
- Manage device permissions through your device settings
- Opt out of certain notifications

Some requests may be limited where information must be retained for legal, safety, security, or operational reasons.

## 11. Children’s Privacy

Pulse Guard is not intended for children who are not legally able to consent to the collection and use of their information.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child without the required consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

## 12. Emergency and Safety Disclaimer

Pulse Guard is not a replacement for emergency services.

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent assistance, contact your local emergency services immediately.

Pulse Guard does not guarantee that a submitted report will result in emergency response, police response, medical response, or any specific action.

## 13. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, we take reasonable steps to protect information according to applicable privacy and data protection laws.

## 14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice through the app or another appropriate channel.

Your continued use of Pulse Guard after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
